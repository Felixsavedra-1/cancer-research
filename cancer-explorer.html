What am I looking at? This tool downloads the real predicted 3D structure of a protein from the AlphaFold database and draws it in your browser. Cancer is often driven by mutations that change a single building block (amino acid) of a protein. Type a mutation like R175H to see exactly where on the protein it lands — mutations buried in the protein's core often break its function.

Scope: single-chain (monomer) models only; pockets come from a LIGSITE-style geometric scan, not a chemistry-aware docking method; folding dynamics are down-weighted by AlphaFold pLDDT confidence. The ranking rediscovers known biology but is not clinically validated — research and education use only.
